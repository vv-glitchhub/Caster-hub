# Caster Workspace Pages

This document tracks the first workspace split for Carcaster and Travelcaster.

## Carcaster

Main page:

- `/apps/carcaster`

Workspace pages:

- `/apps/carcaster/faults`
- `/apps/carcaster/maintenance`

Purpose:

- Faults page separates engine, parking sensor and camera issues.
- Maintenance page tracks planned service, completed work and estimated ownership costs.

Next workspace pages:

- `/apps/carcaster/profile`
- `/apps/carcaster/costs`
- `/apps/carcaster/messages`
- `/apps/carcaster/reminders`

## Travelcaster

Main page:

- `/apps/travelcaster`

Workspace pages:

- `/apps/travelcaster/itinerary`
- `/apps/travelcaster/budget`

Purpose:

- Itinerary page builds a realistic day plan.
- Budget page tracks spend by category.

Next workspace pages:

- `/apps/travelcaster/trips`
- `/apps/travelcaster/packing`
- `/apps/travelcaster/places`
- `/apps/travelcaster/routes`

## Direction

The app pages are moving from landing pages into real product workspaces. The next step is persistent data and forms.
